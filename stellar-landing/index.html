FAQ

Questions, answered.

How long does setup take?

Most businesses are up and running in under an hour. Our team can also assist with full data migration from your existing systems at no extra cost on Growth and Enterprise plans.

Can I migrate data from my current system?

Yes — we provide migration tools and assistance for moving data from Excel, QuickBooks, Tally, Zoho, SAP, and most popular ERPs.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption, multi-tenant data isolation, role-based access, daily backups, and follow industry-standard security practices. Enterprise plans include compliance certifications.

Can I use Stellar offline?

Stellar is cloud-first for real-time sync, but core POS and inventory functions work offline and sync when you reconnect. We also offer self-hosted options for Enterprise customers.

What if I need a custom module?

Our team can build custom modules for your specific workflow on Growth and Enterprise plans. We've built everything from custom manufacturing pipelines to specialized accounting integrations.

Do you offer training?

Yes! Every plan includes onboarding sessions, video tutorials, and documentation. Growth and Enterprise customers get personalized training for their team.
